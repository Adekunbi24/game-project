Grade 4 - Words and Opposite

Welcome!

Before we dive in you should understand what antonyms and synonyms are!

Antonym is a word that mean the opposite of another word. For instance the antonym of 'hot' may be 'cold'
In order to understand antonyms lets look at the meaning of synonyms. A synonym is a word that has a similar
meaning to or exactly the same meaning as another word. Synonyms and Antonyms are words and opposite as well.

Why are Antonyms important

Knowing opposites of words can help us compare and contrast different concepts and organize the vocabulary in our brain.
For example, if we say it is not small and not short, we can assume that the object in question is big and tall if we know
the antonyms for each word.

Nouns can be antonyms(for example; courage and cowardice) as can be verbs (arrive and depart) adverbs (carefully and carelessly) and even prepositions (above and below)

Examples of Antonyms in a sentence:

(1) When it is cold and wet outside, it is nice to be warm and dry inside.
(2) I was raised in Texas, so I play a lot of summer sports and not many winter sports
(3) I can be friends with either a short guy or a tall guy, what matters is if he is friendly.
